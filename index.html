  • Web Project

    Helping NGO

    Situation when, People are willing to help street animals that have been involved in accidents, but are unable to do so because they do not have any contact information for veterinary doctors or animal rescue NGOs. As a result, these animals often die without receiving any assistance. To address this issue, we have created a website that allows people to register the location and provide an image or short video of the injured animal. Once this information is submitted, any nearby veterinary doctor or animal rescue NGO will be notified and can reach the location to provide necessary help and rescue. This website serves as a platform to connect people who want to help injured animals with the relevant organizations that can provide assistance.
